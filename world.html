Beach House House It is located in Malibu, California, and Barbie and her sisters Skipper, Stacie, and Chelsea live there.
Wings and Things Retail It is located in Gloss Angeles and is owned by Reena and her husband Graylen, a human and a fairy respectively. The primary purpose of the store is to provide fake wings to humans in the fairy world.
Princess Graciella's Palace Palace It is at the top of Gloss Angeles, and Princess Graciella lives in it with Zane, Crystal and her assistants. All the major events in the movie take place here.
Gloss Angeles City It is a fairy city currently ruled by Princess Graciella and her husband Prince Zane. In order to get to Gloss Angeles, a fairy needs to make a portal, or they can use fairy flyways. Barbie, Raquelle, Carrie and Taylor use a flyway to go to Paris and use Lilliana Roxelle's portal to Gloss Angeles because it was the only one the princess did not seal.
